Modeling the Orbits of Jupiter's Galliean Satellites

Project for Astro 120 — Optical and Infrared Astronomy Lab.
Using a ~3hr observation taken with the Leushner 30 inch telescope, we developed models of the moon orbits by fitting parametric equations with MCMC.
