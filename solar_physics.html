Improving MagPy

I was a participant in the 2021 University of Alabama in Huntsville and NASA Marshall Space Flight Center Heliophysics Summer Research Experience for Undergraduates. I worked with Dr. David Falconer to correct the radial distance dependence of magnetic measures in JSOC deprojected cylindrical magnetograms. My work is in the process of being incorporated into MagPy, an improved and Python-based version of MAG4, to improve forecasts far from disk center. From my poster and talk, I won first place in the program's poster competition. I presented this poster at the AGU 2021 Fall Meeting in New Orleans, LA and at COSPAR 2022 in Athens, Greece!
