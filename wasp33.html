Detecting Exoplanet WASP-33b

Project for Astro 120 — Optical and Infrared Astronomy Lab.

We observed WASP-33 on October 11, 2021 from 1:34 - 4:30am PST using the Leuschner telescope. Using the transit method, we were able to detect planet WASP-33b and determine its radius to be 1.80 ± 0.49 RJ.

Ratio of WASP-33 and reference star flux as a function of time. Normalized so that the weighted average of pre and post transit is 1.
