Characterizing the Time Evolution of Free-Energy Proxies

Participant in the Solar and Heliospheric Physics REU Program jointly organized by UAH/CSPAR and NASA/MSFC. I worked with Dr. David Falconer to correct the radial distance dependence of gradient and shear weighted neutral line measures in JSOC deprojected cylindrical magnetograms.

I won first place in the 2021 UAH/CSPAR REU Poster Competition. I presented this poster at the AGU 2021 Fall Meeting, and I'll also be presenting at a TBD international conference!
