Hi There!

I'm Ayla (pronounced eye-la) and I'm a senior at UC Berkeley studying Astrophysics!

I'm passionate about data analysis and data visualization. The work I've done includes improving MagPy, one of NASA's solar weather forecasting softwares, and refining Type Ia supernovae coordinates.

I also teach! I'm a GSI for Astronomy C10, Introduction to General Astronomy, taught by Dr. Alex Filippenko, and I'm an instructor for the student-run Python DeCal at Berkeley.

When I'm not coding, teaching, or thinking about space, you'll probably find me backpacking, cooking for my co-op, or tending to my plants.
